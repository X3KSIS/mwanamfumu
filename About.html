About Us

Your trusted partner in construction, equipment supply, and mining accessories.

Mwana Mfumu Contractors Zambia Limited is a leading provider of comprehensive solutions in the construction, equipment supply, and mining industries. With a strong commitment to quality, safety, and innovation, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in delivering excellence across all our projects. Our team of experienced professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of quality. Whether you need road construction, infrastructure development, or specialized mining accessories, we are here to meet your needs and exceed your expectations.

Our Aim

To be the most reliable and efficient provider of construction, equipment, and mining solutions in Zambia.

Our mission is to deliver top-quality services and products that meet the evolving needs of our clients while maintaining the highest standards of safety and sustainability. We strive to foster long-term relationships with our clients by consistently providing innovative solutions and exceptional service.
